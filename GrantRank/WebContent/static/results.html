About GoodCity

Goodcity is a Human Investment Organization. For over 30 years, Goodcity has been committed to starting, growing, and "investing" in entrepreneurs who are making a difference in under-resourced urban communities. We work with both nonprofit organizations and for-profit organizations who promote self-sufficiency, hope and purpose in the communities they are a part of.
